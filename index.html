Hello! My name is Joe, and I'm an education reporter based in Southern California.

I currently cover three public school districts and one community college at The Desert Sun, a daily newspaper in Palm Springs, CA.

I've written across the ed beat at federal, state and local levels, but my first commitment has always been to cultivating relationships with sources. I'm a stickler for attending board meetings, parent events and high school football games to get the stories that the data might miss.

I've used data throughout my career, but I'm currently working to expand my technical skill set. In July 2019, I attended The Data Institute in New York City, a two-week training in data journalism taught by reporters at ProPublica.
